# First Login

Welcome to JCTC! This guide will help you log in and set up your account.

---

## Step 1: Access the Login Page

Navigate to your JCTC instance URL (e.g., `https://jctc.ng`).

![Login Page](../assets/screenshots/login.jpg)

---

## Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

1. **Email**: Enter the email address provided by your administrator
2. **Password**: Enter your temporary password

!!! tip "First-time users"
    If this is your first login, use the temporary password sent to your email.
    You will be prompted to change it immediately.

---

## Step 3: Two-Factor Authentication (if enabled)

If your organization has enabled 2FA:

1. Open your authenticator app
2. Enter the 6-digit code
3. Click **Verify**

---

## Step 4: Complete Your Profile

After your first login, complete your profile:

1. Click your **avatar** in the top-right corner
2. Select **Profile Settings**
3. Fill in your details:
    - Full Name
    - Phone Number
    - Department
4. Click **Save Changes**
