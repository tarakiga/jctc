# Add Parties to Case

Accurately recording the people involved in a case is essential for investigation and legal proceedings. The **Parties** module tracks Suspects, Victims, Witnesses, and Complainants.

## Adding a New Party
1. Open the Case and select the **Parties & Individuals** tab.
2. Click **Add Party**.
3. **Select Party Type**:
    *   **Suspect**: Individual under investigation.
    *   **Victim**: Individual harmed by the alleged crime.
    *   **Witness**: Individual providing testimony or evidence.
    *   **Complainant**: The person/entity who reported the case.

### Required Information
*   **Full Name**: Legal name of the individual.
*   **Party Type**: As selected above.
*   **Guardian Info**: **Required for Victims under 18**. You must provide a guardian's name and contact.

### Optional Details
*   **Alias/Username**: Known nicknames or online handles (critical for cybercrime).
*   **Date of Birth**: Used to calculate age and determine minor status automatically.
*   **Nationality**: Citizenship status.
*   **Contact Info**: Phone, Email, Address.
*   **Safeguarding Flags**: (See below).

## Safeguarding Flags (Victims)
When adding a **Victim**, you can tag specific safeguarding needs. These flags highlight the profile for attention:
- **Medical**: Requires medical attention.
- **Shelter**: Requires safe housing.
- **Counselling**: Requires psychological support.
- **Legal Aid**: Requires legal representation assistance.

## Managing Parties
*   **Filter**: Use the dropdown at the top to view only Suspects, Victims, etc.
*   **Edit**: Click the actions menu (three dots) on a party row to update details.
*   **Delete**: Remove a party entered in error.
