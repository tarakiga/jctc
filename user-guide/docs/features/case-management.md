# Case Management Detail

The **Case Detail** view is the central hub for managing an investigation. It provides a comprehensive set of tools to track every aspect of a case, from evidence and parties to legal instruments and prosecution.

## Accessing Case Detail
To open a case:
1. Navigate to the **Cases** list from the sidebar.
2. Click on any case row or the case number to open the detailed view.

## Interface Overview
The Case Detail view is divided into several sections, navigable via tabs or the sidebar menu:

### Core Management
- **Case Overview**: High-level summary, risk flags, quick stats, and metadata.
- **Parties & Individuals**: Manage suspects, victims, witnesses, and complainants.
- **Team Assignments**: Assign investigators and manage access rights.
- **Tasks**: Track investigative to-dos and deadlines.
- **Action Log**: Audit trail of all case activities (automatic and manual).
- **Case Timeline**: Visual chronology of key case events.

### Evidence & Forensics
- **Evidence Management**: Detailed tracking of digital and physical evidence (Chain of Custody).
- **Seizures**: Record physical raid and seizure operations.
- **Forensic Analysis**: Manage artefacts and forensic reports.

### Legal & Prosecution
- **Legal Instruments**: Manage warrants, court orders, and preservation requests.
- **Prosecution**: Track charges, court sessions, and case outcomes.
- **International Cooperation**: Manage MLATs and cross-border requests.

### Collaboration
- **Attachments**: General file storage for the case.
- **Collaboration**: Shared notes and team communication.

## Key Sections Breakdown

### 1. Case Overview
Provides a dashboard-style summary:
- **Case Description**: Full narrative of the case.
- **Investigation Details**: Type, date reported, lead investigator, and scope.
- **Intake & Reporter**: Source of the case and reporter contact info.
- **Risk Flags**: Critical indicators like "Child Safety," "Imminent Harm," or "Trafficking."
- **Stats**: Quick counts of evidence, days open, and team members.

### 2. Evidence Management
The core module for digital and physical evidence.
- **Add Evidence**: Log new items.
- **Chain of Custody**: Track every movement and handover of evidence.
- **verification**: Hash verification for digital assets.
[Learn more about Evidence Tracking](../tutorials/manage-evidence.md) | [Chain of Custody](../tutorials/track-chain-of-custody.md)

### 3. Seizures
Manage physical operations and raids.
- **Log Seizure**: Record location, date, removing officer, and legal authority.
- **Link Evidence**: Associate individual evidence items with specific seizure operations.
[Learn more about Seizures](../tutorials/manage-seizures.md)

### 4. Parties & Individuals
Manage all people involved in the case.
- **Roles**: Distinct tracking for Suspects, Victims, Witnesses, and Complainants.
- **Safeguarding**: Flag vulnerable individuals (minors, medical needs).
[Learn more about Adding Parties](../tutorials/add-parties-to-case.md)

### 5. Legal Instruments
Track the legal basis for investigation.
- **Warrants**: Search & seizure warrants.
- **Court Orders**: Production orders, preservation orders.
- **Expirations**: Alerts for expiring warrants.
[Learn more about Legal Instruments](../tutorials/legal-instruments.md)

### 6. Prosecution
Manage the legal proceedings.
- **Charges**: File charges under specific acts (e.g., Cybercrimes Act 2015).
- **Court Sessions**: Schedule and track hearings and trials.
- **Outcomes**: Record convictions, acquittals, or plea bargains.
[Learn more about Prosecution](../tutorials/prosecution-workflow.md)
