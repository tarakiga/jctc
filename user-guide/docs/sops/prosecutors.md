# Standard Operating Procedures: Prosecutors

**Role**: `PROSECUTOR`  
**Purpose**: To manage the judicial phase of cases, ensuring legal sufficiency and successful prosecution.

## Daily Checklist
1.  **Court Dates**: Check the calendar for today's hearings.
2.  **Case Reviews**: Check for cases marked "Ready for Prosecution" by investigators.
3.  **Filings**: Monitor deadlines for filing charges or motions.

## Core Workflows

### 1. Case Sufficiency Review
When a case is forwarded for prosecution:
1.  Review the **Evidence** tab for chain of custody integrity.
2.  Review **Witness Statements** in the Parties tab.
3.  **Decision**:
    *   *Accept*: Proceed to filing charges.
    *   *Kickback*: Return to investigator with specific notes on missing evidence.

### 2. Filing Charges
1.  Go to the **Prosecution** tab.
2.  Click **File Charge**.
3.  Select the specific **Act** and **Section** (e.g., Cybercrimes Act 2015).
4.  Copy the specific legal text into the description if needed.

### 3. Court Management
1.  **Schedule**: Log every hearing in the **Court Sessions** panel.
2.  **Updates**: After every session, update the session notes (e.g., *Adjourned to [Date]*).
3.  **Outcome**: When judgment is delivered, record the **Case Outcome** (Conviction, Acquittal, etc.).

## Critical Compliance
!!! important "Disclosure"
    *   Ensure all exculpatory evidence is disclosed to the defense as per legal requirements.
    *   Verify the physical location of evidence before any trial date.
