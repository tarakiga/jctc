# Standard Operating Procedures: Investigators

**Role**: `INVESTIGATOR`  
**Purpose**: To manage the end-to-end investigation lifecycle, from case assignment to evidence gathering and compliance.

## Daily Checklist (Start of Shift)
1.  **Check Notifications**: Review alerts for new case assignments or task deadlines.
2.  **Review Calendar**: Check for scheduled interviews, court appearances, or team briefings.
3.  **Priorities**: Filter **My Cases** by "High Severity" or "Due Soon" to focus efforts.

## Core Workflows

### 1. New Case Assignment
When a supervisor assigns a case to you:
1.  Review the **Case Overview** and **Intake Report**.
2.  Assess the **Risk Flags** (e.g., *Imminent Harm*).
3.  Go to the **Plan** tab (if available) or create a **Task** list for initial steps.
4.  **Action**: Contact the complainant within 24 hours to verify details.

### 2. Evidence Collection
When seizing devices or collecting physical evidence:
1.  **Safety First**: Ensure the scene is secure.
2.  **Log Seizure**: Use the **Seizures** module to record the location and time.
3.  **Tag & Bag**: Physically tag the item.
4.  **Digital Entry**: Immediately register the item in the **Evidence** tab.
    *   *Critical*: Record the specific serial number.
5.  **Chain of Custody**: Adding the initial entry is mandatory. Set "From" as *Location/Owner* and "To" as *Yourself*.

### 3. Requesting Warrants
If legal authority is needed:
1.  Draft the affidavit offline.
2.  Go to **Legal Instruments**.
3.  Click **Add Instrument**.
4.  Upload the draft application or signed warrant.
5.  **Status**: Mark as *Requested* until signed by a judge.

## Critical Compliance
!!! danger "Zero Tolerance"
    *   **Never** share your login credentials.
    *   **Always** lock your workstation when stepping away.
    *   **Do not** delete evidence records without written authorization.
    *   **Report** any conflict of interest immediately to your supervisor.
